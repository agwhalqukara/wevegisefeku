    Pot, meet kettle and Camille Grammer!

    On part two of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills reunion, host Andy Cohen asks Grammer if she was hypocritical to bring up Faye Resnick's 1997 Playboy spread considering her own past in soft-core pornography.

    The soon-to-be ex-Mrs. Grammer, 42, shrugs off the accusation, claiming Resnick, "gave that fake smile. I like people to be sincere." 

    Grammer goes on to call Resnick "morally corrupt" for posing nude three years after writing a book about her friend Nicole Brown Simpson's murder.
