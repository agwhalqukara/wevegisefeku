    News: As of 2023, Mike Murdock, the American singer-songwriter and televangelist, is believed to have a net worth of $6 million. Additionally, he serves as the pastor of The Wisdom Center ministry located in Haltom City, Texas. Renowned for promoting prosperity theology, Murdock delivers lectures on a global scale and leads the TV program “Anointed School of Wisdom with Mike Murdock.”

    Career and Income

    Murdock, born on April 18, 1946, in Lake Charles, Louisiana, has enjoyed a prosperous career as a contemporary Christian singer-songwriter, televangelist, and pastor. His annual earnings are approximated to surpass $600,000, accompanied by a monthly income exceeding $60,000.

    Net Worth

    Murdock’s financial standing has experienced notable expansion over the years. As of 2023, his estimated net worth has reached $6 million, marking a steady increase from $5.4 million in 2022, $4.8 million in 2021, $4.2 million in 2020, and $3.6 million in 2019.

    Early Life and Education

    Murdock’s path to success commenced in his birthplace of Lake Charles, Louisiana. He graduated from LaGrange High School in 1964. Subsequently, he pursued higher education at Southwestern Assemblies of God University, but his academic journey was cut short when he decided to discontinue after three semesters in 1966.

    Controversies

    Over the course of his career, Murdock has officially registered 76 songs with ASCAP. In 1989, he garnered attention for his role as a pastor on The PTL Club, where he urged viewers to contribute to Jim and Tammy Faye Bakker. Nevertheless, the PTL faced financial collapse, and Jim Bakker was subsequently incarcerated for fraud.

    Luxurious Lifestyle

    In addition to his thriving career, Murdock has gained recognition for his opulent lifestyle. He possesses a lavish residence valued at $1.5 million, featuring 25 rooms and occupying 6.89 acres. Initially listed for sale in November 2011 at $3.2 million, the property underwent a price reduction to $2.7 million in 2013.

    Personal Life

    In 1966, Murdock entered matrimony with Linda Lormand, and together they adopted a son named Jason. Unfortunately, their marriage concluded in 1979 after 13 years, leading to divorce, with Linda being awarded custody of Jason. Since that time, Murdock has not entered into another marriage.

    The Wisdom Center

    In 2004, Murdock initiated the establishment of his own church, named The Wisdom Center, situated in Haltom City, Texas. His presence extends to numerous television networks, and he serves as the host of the show “Wisdom Keys with Mike Murdock.”
