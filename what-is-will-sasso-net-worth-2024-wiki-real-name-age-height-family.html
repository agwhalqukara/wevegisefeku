Sasso was on his way to becoming a comedy icon.

    FAQs about Will Sasso

    Who is Will Sasso?

    Ah, the basics! He is a Canadian actor, comedian, and podcaster known for his spot-on celebrity impersonations and knack for physical comedy. He shot to fame as a cast member on MADtv, where his larger-than-life characters and sketches were nothing short of comedic gold.

    What made him famous?

    While he had been on the scene for a while, it was his stint on MADtv from 1997 to 2002 that really put him on the map. His impersonations of celebs like Arnold Schwarzenegger and his original character, The Eracist, among others, are legendary. Oh, and let’s not forget his stint as Curly in The Three Stooges movie—pure comedic brilliance!

    Has he done any serious roles?

    Yep, our boy can flex his acting muscles beyond comedy! He’s taken on more dramatic roles in movies like Southland Tales and TV shows such as Shameless. It’s always a treat to see him showcase his range.
