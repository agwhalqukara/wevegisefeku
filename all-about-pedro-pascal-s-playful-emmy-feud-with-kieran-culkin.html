    All About Pedro Pascals Playful Emmy Feud with Kieran Culkin

    Pedro Pascal, the charismatic star of Last of Us, brought more than just an award to the 2024 Emmys. His wit and humor took the spotlight as he stepped on stage to present the accolade for Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.

    26 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Pedro Pascal, the charismatic star of “Last of Us,” brought more than just an award to the 2024 Emmys. His wit and humor took the spotlight as he stepped on stage to present the accolade for Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series.

    However, it wasn’t the award that stole the show; it was Pascal’s ongoing faux feud with “Succession” actor Kieran Culkin that had the audience in stitches.

    Sporting a sling and adding a dash of mystery to his appearance, Pascal addressed the elephant in the room right away.

    “A lot of people have been asking about my arm,” he quipped, immediately capturing everyone’s attention. “It’s my shoulder. And I think tonight is a perfect time to tell everyone that Kieran Culkin beat the s––t out of me,” Pascal declared, with a mischievous glint in his eye.

    However, the censors had other plans, bleeping out Pascal’s colorful language, leaving viewers to imagine the exact words he chose to describe his encounter with Culkin.

    The camera cut to Culkin, initially unamused but unable to resist breaking into a smile moments later. The tension between the two actors, brewing since the Golden Globes, added a delightful layer of unpredictability to the evening.

    The saga between Pascal and Culkin began at the Golden Globes earlier in the month, where Culkin took home the award for Best Lead Actor in a Drama Series for “Succession.”

    Seizing the moment, Culkin playfully taunted Pascal, who was also nominated in the same category. “Suck it, Pedro! Sorry. Mine!” Culkin exclaimed in his acceptance speech, igniting the spark for their lighthearted rivalry.
